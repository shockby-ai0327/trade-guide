## J類：展覽與開發新客戶（Trade Shows & New Business）

### J-001
**場景**：在汽車零件展（如法蘭克福Automechanika）上，一個陌生的美國買家走近你的展台。
**開場白**：
> "Welcome! I'm [Name] from Dongguan Hirosawa — we specialize in automotive interior and exterior trim, from chrome plating to real wood finishing. Are you sourcing for a specific vehicle model or application? [Hand over product sample] Feel free to touch and feel the quality — that's what sets us apart."

---

### J-002
**場景**：展覽上客戶問：「你們跟你隔壁那家有什麼不一樣？」
**你的回應**：
> "Great question. Three key differentiators:
> 1. OEM-grade quality: We supply Toyota, Honda, and Mercedes-Benz production lines — not just aftermarket. That means tighter tolerances and zero-defect mindset.
> 2. Surface engineering expertise: Very few companies handle chrome, real wood, in-mold printing, AND painting under one roof. We do.
> 3. Local presence in the US and Mexico — faster response, local stocking option for North American customers.
> What's your key pain point with your current supplier?"

---

### J-003
**場景**：展覽上你拿到了潛在客戶的名片，展後如何跟進？
**展後跟進郵件（展後48小時內）**：
> "Subject: Following up from Automechanika [Year] | Hirosawa Automotive Trim
> 
> Hi [Name], 
> It was a pleasure meeting you at Automechanika Frankfurt. I hope you had a productive show!
> As discussed, we specialize in [specific product they were interested in]. I've attached our catalog and a brief company profile for your reference.
> To take the next step, I'd suggest a 20-minute video call to understand your specific requirements. Would [date/time options] work for you?
> Looking forward to connecting further."

---

### J-004
**場景**：客戶在展覽上提到他有個競爭對手供應商的問題（比如品質不穩定）。如何抓住機會？
**你的回應**：
> "I hear that quality consistency has been a challenge. This is actually one of the most common reasons new clients come to us. 
> Our approach: every batch undergoes 100% dimensional check on critical features + statistical AQL sampling. For your specific application, what are the 2-3 quality features that matter most?
> I'd suggest sending you a set of our samples so you can compare directly. No cost, no commitment — just give your engineering team a chance to evaluate. Interested?"

---

### J-005
**場景**：在LinkedIn上開發新客戶，如何發第一條訊息？
**LinkedIn First Message**：
> "Hi [Name], I noticed you're in automotive sourcing at [Company]. I'm with Dongguan Hirosawa — we've been supplying Toyota/Honda-spec interior and exterior trim for 25+ years, and we recently expanded to North American service.
> Given your focus on [their industry/role], I thought there might be a fit. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call? No hard sell — just to understand if what we offer is relevant to your work."

---

### J-006
**場景**：你收到了一封「Cold RFQ」（陌生詢價），對方不知名。如何評估是否值得回？
**評估清單**：
1. 公司名稱 → Google搜索，是否有網站、LinkedIn、聯繫地址？
2. 詢價的產品是否在你們能力範圍內？
3. 數量是否合理？（1件詢價 vs 10,000件詢價）
4. 付款方式是否可接受？（對方說Net 90嗎？）
5. 是否有其他危險信號？（Gmail帳戶、無公司地址、異常急單）

**回應原則**：即使不確定，簡短回應以收集更多信息，不要直接報價。

---

### J-007
**場景**：客戶在詢價郵件中同時比較了你和3個競爭對手，要求「best price by Friday」。
**你的回應**：
> "Thank you for including Hirosawa in your RFQ process. Before submitting a price, I have a few quick questions to ensure I'm quoting the right specification:
> [list 2-3 technical questions]
> I can provide our most competitive pricing by [Friday], and I'd also like to briefly discuss our unique value proposition — there are aspects of our service that don't show in a price comparison but can significantly reduce your total cost of ownership.
> Would 15 minutes Thursday work?"

---

### J-008
**場景**：你要做一個「Company Introduction」，對方是個大型歐洲採購商。
**話術模板**：
> "Good morning, [Name] and team. Thank you for this opportunity to present Hirosawa.
> 
> Let me start with who we are: We're a Tier 1 automotive trim supplier established in 1999, headquartered in Dongguan, China — the heart of China's manufacturing ecosystem. With 3,500 employees and plants in China, USA, and Mexico, we serve the full spectrum of automotive customers from Toyota and Honda to Mercedes-Benz and Volkswagen.
> 
> What makes us different: Most trim suppliers specialize in one process. We have six surface engineering technologies under one roof — chrome, painting, real wood, in-mold printing, PVD, and electroplating. This integration means shorter supply chains, better quality control, and faster turnaround for you.
> 
> What I'd like to understand from you today: What are your current supply challenges? What's working, what's not? Let's see if there's a genuine fit."

---

### J-009
**場景**：客戶問「你們有沒有做過北美市場？」你需要強調美國/墨西哥布局。
**你的回應**：
> "Yes, North America is a key market for us. We established Hirosawa Automotive Trim USA to serve North American customers with:
> - Local warehousing for fast replenishment
> - US-based technical support
> - Mexico manufacturing (compliant with USMCA) for tariff-sensitive items
> We currently supply [several] US-based distributors and aftermarket retailers. I'd be happy to connect you with references if that would help."

---

### J-010
**場景**：客戶問「你們有沒有做過HELLA/VALEO這種Tier 1的供應商？」
**你的回應**：
> "We primarily supply directly to OEMs (Toyota, Honda, etc.) as a Tier 1 supplier. For the Tier 1 component supplier market (like HELLA, Valeo, or Magna), we've worked with several on specific decoration/trim sub-assembly projects.
> Our experience with Tier 1 OEM quality standards means we meet the stringent PPAP, APQP, and SPC requirements that Tier 1 component makers typically demand. What specific requirements does your customer have?"

## L類：隨機條件情境題（Random Condition Scenarios）

以下情境隨機組合了**市場、產品、付款方式、客戶類型、特殊條件**，模擬真實不可預測情況：

### L-001
**條件**：德國客戶 × 信用狀 × 要求即期信用狀 × 產品：真木飾板 × 數量：2,000pcs × 交期問題：你們廠師傅請假，短缺人力
**情境**：德國客戶L/C到了，但是你的真木工藝部門師傅請假，可能延誤10天。
**你的應對**：
> 1. 立刻評估L/C最遲裝船日是否允許延遲
> 2. 如果不行，通知客戶申請裝船日期Amendment
> 3. 考慮是否可以從其他廠調貨或加班趕工
> 4. 對客戶說：
> "I need to flag an urgent production issue — our specialized wood-grain craftsmen have unexpected absences, which may delay your shipment by approximately 10 days. 
> Current latest shipment date on L/C: [date]. Our revised ETA: [new date].
> Please arrange an amendment extending the latest shipment date to [new date]. I sincerely apologize for this disruption."

### L-002
**條件**：美國客戶 × T/T × 新客戶 × 產品：電鍍格柵 × 訂單金額很大（USD 150,000）× 客戶要求Net 60付款

### L-010
**條件**：台灣客戶 × 問能否替他們代工然後銷往美國 × 但美國對中國產品有關稅 × 客戶問能否標示「Made in Taiwan」
**情境**：客戶要求改變原產地標示逃避關稅。

**你的應對**：

> 2. Substantial transformation — if Taiwan adds sufficient value (typically 40%+ of value added), Taiwan origin may be legitimately claimed

> 
> We cannot assist with any false origin marking. But I'm happy to explore the legal options with you."

### L-013
**條件**：你同時跟進5個客戶，其中一個催得很急，另外4個在等待中。如何管理時間？
**時間管理框架**：
1. **緊急+重要**（收款風險、出貨卡關）→ 立刻處理
2. **不急+重要**（新客戶開發、大訂單跟進）→ 排進今天下午
3. **緊急+不重要**（客戶問一個簡單問題）→ 2分鐘內回複或委派
4. **不急+不重要**（舊catalog更新）→ 本週內
> 原則：每天早上先發一封簡短狀態更新給所有在途訂單客戶，主動告知進度，把被動催促轉為主動溝通。

> We will take full responsibility for any confirmed manufacturing defects. A senior representative from our team will contact you directly within [X] hours."

## M類：合規與法律情境（Compliance & Legal）

**你的回應**：
> "We note the governing law clause. For our standard international contracts, we prefer:
> - Governing law: People's Republic of China law, OR
> - Neutral: ICC Arbitration, seat in Hong Kong (or Singapore)
> 
> California law would require us to retain local US legal counsel for any disputes, which is costly and complex. Could we discuss a mutually neutral arbitration venue?"

---

### M-002
**場景**：客戶要求簽署他們公司的標準採購條款（General Terms and Conditions of Purchase），其中有很多對廣澤不利的條款。
**你的回應**：
> "Thank you for sharing your GTC. Our legal team has reviewed them and we'd like to propose amendments to a few clauses:
> - Clause [X] on liability cap: We'd like to cap our liability at the value of the specific order, not cumulative
> - Clause [X] on IP ownership: Design IP developed by Hirosawa remains our property unless specifically agreed in writing
> - Clause [X] on payment terms: Our standard terms supersede this
> Could we schedule a call to align on the key commercial terms?"

---

### M-003
**場景**：新客戶想做試單，但要求先簽一個「Exclusivity Agreement」——在他們市場不賣給任何人。
**你的回應**：
> "Exclusivity is a significant commercial commitment. We'd need to see volume commitments that justify limiting our market access in [territory].
> For a trial order, let's start without exclusivity — once you've confirmed the product meets your requirements and you've placed [X] orders totaling USD [amount], we can revisit an exclusivity arrangement with a formal minimum purchase commitment.
> Does this phased approach work for you?"

---

### M-004
**場景**：客戶問你們有沒有做過「CSR（企業社會責任）」報告，有沒有勞工標準認證。

### M-005
**場景**：你懷疑對方是詐騙（Fraud）——郵件要求更改銀行帳號，說之前的帳號出問題了。
**策略**：這是常見的「Business Email Compromise（BEC）」詐騙！
**你的應對**：
> 絕對不要按新帳號匯款！
> 1. 通過電話（已知的真實電話）直接確認
> 2. 回覆到你**之前知道的**郵件地址（不是那封新郵件的回覆）
> 3. 通知你的財務部門凍結該筆款項
>
> 對客戶說（電話）：
> "Hi [Name], I just received an email from what appears to be your email address requesting a change of bank account. Before we proceed with any payment, I want to verbally confirm with you directly — is this a legitimate request? We're seeing a lot of business email fraud and want to be absolutely sure."

## N類：中國內部協調情境（Internal Coordination in China）

### N-001
**場景**：你的工廠（廣澤）和你的老闆說不能接這個訂單，因為產能不夠。但你已經跟客戶承諾了。
**你的應對（內部）**：
> 分析後提出方案：
> 1. 能否加班/夜班解決？
> 2. 能否讓另一個廠區支援？
> 3. 能否延後交期後接單？
> 先解決內部，再對客戶說：
> "I need to revise our delivery commitment — due to current production loading, we can now commit to [new date]. I sincerely apologize for this change. We will prioritize your order once the slot opens."

---

### N-002
**場景**：工廠說這個客戶的圖紙要求太高，工藝做不到，但客戶已付了訂金。
**你的應對**：
> "We've completed a detailed feasibility assessment of your specification [drawing number]. We've identified one challenge:
> [Specific feature] requires a tolerance of ±0.1mm, which is at the limit of our current process capability. Our Cpk for this dimension is approximately 1.1 (minimum is 1.33 for critical features).
> Options:
> 1. Can the tolerance be relaxed to ±0.15mm? This is within our Cpk 1.67 range.
> 2. We invest in process improvement — adds 2 weeks lead time, no cost to you
> 3. Full refund of deposit if you'd prefer to seek an alternative supplier
> We want to be upfront about our capabilities rather than deliver substandard parts. Your thoughts?"

---

### N-003
**場景**：海外客戶用的是WeChat跟你溝通，而不是Email。如何處理重要確認？
**你的應對**：
> WeChat適合：快速溝通、確認小事、追蹤狀態
> Email必須用於：報價確認、PI確認、正式投訴、技術規格確認
>
> 對客戶說：
> "I enjoy our WeChat conversations! For our official records, could we also confirm the key details over email? This protects both of us in case there are any disputes or reference needs later. I'll send you a summary email of today's discussion — just reply to confirm."

### N-004
**場景**：你的公司有一個不同語言的部門（中國工程師不懂英文），客戶要和工程師直接溝通。
**你的應對**：
> "Our engineering team handles communication primarily in Chinese. I'd be happy to serve as the interface between you and our engineers.
> For technical discussions, please share your questions/requirements in writing and I'll:
> 1. Translate and relay to our engineering team
> 2. Convey their response back to you with full accuracy
> 3. For complex technical reviews, we can arrange a video call with me as interpreter
> Alternatively, if you have a mandarin-speaking engineer on your team, we can arrange a direct technical call."

### N-005
**場景**：客戶想要做「工廠審核（Factory Audit/Supplier Audit）」，你需要準備什麼？
**工廠審核準備清單**：
> 告知客戶並準備：
> 1. 質量手冊（Quality Manual）
> 2. IATF 16949證書
> 3. 生產流程圖（Process Flow）
> 4. PFMEA（工藝失效模式分析）
> 5. 控制計劃（Control Plan）
> 6. 主要客戶清單（已脫敏）
> 7. 近期Cpk/PPM數據
> 8. 員工培訓記錄
> 9. 5S現場管理展示
> 10. 環保/安全記錄
>
> 對客戶說："We welcome your audit. Typical duration is one day. Please share your audit checklist in advance so we can prepare all documentation. We suggest visiting on [date] to also see an active production run."
