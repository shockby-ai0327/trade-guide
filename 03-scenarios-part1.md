# 第三部分：國貿情境模擬（500+題）Part 1：詢價、報價、訂單

> **使用方式**：每個情境包含【場景】【你的應對策略】【英文話術】。請隨機挑選練習，直到反應自然為止。

---

## A類：詢價與報價（Inquiry & Quotation）

### A-001
**場景**：美國客戶發來第一封詢價郵件，要求報A車型前保險桿裝飾條，數量1000pcs，要求FOB報價，交期30天內。
**關鍵點**：需要確認規格、材料、顏色、表面處理方式才能報價。
**策略**：先確認規格，不要盲目報價。
**你的回應**：
> "Thank you for your inquiry. To provide an accurate quotation, could you please share the following details:
> 1. Part drawing / 2D or 3D file
> 2. Material requirement (PP, ABS, or other)
> 3. Surface finish (painting, chrome plating, or printing)
> 4. Target color (RAL or Pantone code preferred)
> 5. Annual estimated volume
> We'll revert with our best price within 3 working days upon receiving the above."

---

### A-002
**場景**：德國客戶詢問你們是否有IATF 16949認證，並要求提供證書。
**策略**：確認公司認證狀況後作答；如果不確定，說「Let me confirm and send you the certificate」，不要亂說有。
**你的回應**：
> "Yes, Dongguan Hirosawa is IATF 16949 certified. I'll send you a copy of our certificate by the end of today. Could you also share your specific quality requirements so we can better prepare for your audit process?"

---

### A-003
**場景**：客戶要求FOB報價，但你們習慣用CIF。
**策略**：可以兩個都報，讓客戶選。
**你的回應**：
> "As requested, our FOB Yantian price is USD X.XX/pc for 1,000 pcs MOQ. For your reference, we can also offer CIF [destination port] at USD X.XX/pc, which includes freight and insurance. Please let us know your preference."

---

### A-004
**場景**：客戶說你的價格比競爭對手高20%，要求降價。
**策略**：不要立刻降，先了解對手報價基礎，然後講差異化。
**你的回應**：
> "We appreciate your transparency. Could you share the competitor's quotation details so we can do an apples-to-apples comparison? 
> Our price reflects: IATF 16949 quality system, Toyota/Honda-level QC standards, and our established track record with Tier 1 OEMs. 
> That said, we can explore volume-based pricing — if you can commit to X,000 pcs annually, we can offer a more competitive rate."

---

### A-005
**場景**：客戶要求樣品，問樣品費多少。
**關鍵點**：樣品費通常客戶先付，量產後從貨款扣除。
**你的回應**：
> "Sample fee is USD XXX per piece, covering tooling/mold setup and production. Once you confirm the bulk order, we'll credit the sample fee against your first production invoice. Shall I proceed with the sample preparation?"

---

### A-006
**場景**：客戶問「你們MOQ是多少？」
**策略**：說出MOQ同時提供靈活方案。
**你的回應**：
> "Our standard MOQ is 500 pcs per item. However, for initial trial orders, we can accommodate 200 pcs with a small-run surcharge of approximately 15%. This allows you to test the market before committing to full volumes."

---

### A-007
**場景**：客戶問能否提供OEM服務（打客戶自己的品牌）。
**你的回應**：
> "Yes, we offer full OEM/ODM service. We can manufacture to your specifications and apply your brand logo. A non-disclosure agreement (NDA) can be signed before we discuss proprietary designs. Please share your design files and we'll evaluate feasibility."

---

### A-008
**場景**：客戶詢問「你們有現貨嗎？」（Stock availability）
**你的回應**：
> "We maintain stock of our standard catalog items. Could you share the part number or description? If it's a custom part, lead time would be X weeks from order confirmation. I'll check our inventory and revert within the hour."

---

### A-009
**場景**：客戶要求報價要「含稅價」（Landed cost），包括美國進口關稅。
**策略**：你可以提供DDP報價，但必須加上關稅。
**你的回應**：
> "To calculate the landed cost to the US, we need to factor in: freight (~USD X/cbm), insurance (0.3%), and US import duty (HS 8708.29 currently at 2.5% + additional Section 301 tariff of 25%). 
> I'd recommend you confirm the applicable tariff rate with your customs broker, as rates can change. Once confirmed, I can provide a DDP price for your evaluation."

---

### A-010
**場景**：客戶說「我們是新公司，能否給賒帳（Net 60）？」
**策略**：新客戶不給賒帳，但給台階下。
**你的回應**：
> "For new business relationships, our standard terms are T/T 30% deposit + 70% before shipment. Once we've completed 3 successful orders together, we'd be happy to discuss extended payment terms. This protects both sides as we build trust in the partnership."

---

### A-011
**場景**：客戶詢價後兩週沒有回應，你要follow up。
**你的回應**：
> "Hi [Name], I hope this finds you well. I wanted to follow up on the quotation I sent on [date] for [product name]. 
> Has your team had a chance to review it? We currently have production capacity available in [month], so if you'd like to move forward, this would be a great time to place an order. 
> Please let me know if you need any clarification or adjustments to the quote."

---

### A-012
**場景**：客戶要求提供公司profile和產能資訊。
**你的回應**：
> "Please find our company profile attached. Key highlights:
> - Established: 1999 | 25+ years in automotive trim
> - Employees: 3,500+
> - Certifications: IATF 16949, ISO 14001
> - Key customers: Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Mercedes-Benz, Volkswagen
> - Plants: Dongguan (HQ), Wuhan, USA, Mexico
> - Annual capacity: [X] million pieces
> Happy to arrange a virtual factory tour if that would be helpful."

---

### A-013
**場景**：客戶要求提供「cost breakdown」（成本拆解），問原材料、人工、利潤各多少。
**策略**：這是常見的壓價手段。不要完全拒絕，但要保護利潤結構。
**你的回應**：
> "We typically provide all-in pricing rather than cost breakdowns, as our integrated manufacturing efficiency is part of our value proposition. 
> However, I can confirm that material cost accounts for approximately 40-50% of the price for this product type. If there's a specific target price you're working toward, please share it and we'll see how we can bridge the gap."

---

### A-014
**場景**：客戶詢問你們的打樣（prototype）能力。
**你的回應**：
> "Yes, we offer both CNC-machined prototypes and injection-molded samples. 
> - CNC prototype: 5-7 business days, good for design verification
> - Injection-molded sample: requires T1 mold (4-6 weeks), closest to production quality
> Which stage are you at in your product development? That will help us recommend the right approach."

---

### A-015
**場景**：客戶問「你們接不接小訂單？」（1個貨櫃以下）
**你的回應**：
> "Yes, we can ship LCL (Less-than-Container Load) for smaller orders. For LCL, we typically recommend a minimum of 1 CBM to keep freight costs reasonable. Orders below this threshold may have higher per-unit freight costs. Would you like a freight estimate for your projected volume?"

## B類：訂單確認與合約（Order Confirmation）

### B-001
**場景**：客戶PO上的數量和你們PI上的不一樣，少了50個。
**策略**：立刻核對，不要靜默接受差異。
**你的回應**：
> "Thank you for your PO [number]. I noticed a discrepancy — our PI [number] shows 1,000 pcs, while your PO shows 950 pcs. Could you please confirm the correct quantity? Once confirmed, I'll issue an updated PI and we'll proceed with production."

---

### B-002
**場景**：客戶PO上要求的交貨條款是DAP，但你們平常做FOB。
**策略**：確認這個條款對廣澤成本的影響再確認。
**你的回應**：
> "Noted. For DAP [destination], we'll need to arrange freight to your warehouse. Could you confirm the full delivery address including zip code? I'll check with our freight forwarder for the shipping cost and revert with an adjusted price accordingly."

---

### B-003
**場景**：客戶PO付款條件寫「Net 90 days」，但你們談的是T/T 30/70。
**策略**：必須糾正，否則等於默認同意賒帳90天。
**你的回應**：
> "Thank you for the PO. I noticed the payment terms on the PO say 'Net 90 days', while our agreed terms were 'T/T 30% deposit + 70% before shipment.' 
> Could you please reissue the PO with the correct payment terms, or provide written confirmation of the agreed terms? We cannot commence production until the payment terms are aligned."

---

### B-004
**場景**：客戶要你在PI上把單價拆低（undervalue）以逃避進口關稅。
**策略**：這是走私/逃稅行為，你必須拒絕！
**你的回應**：
> "I appreciate our business relationship, but I'm afraid we cannot issue invoices with undervalued amounts. This would constitute customs fraud, which carries serious legal consequences for both parties in multiple jurisdictions. 
> Our invoice must reflect the true transaction value. I'd suggest consulting with your customs broker — there may be legal duty mitigation strategies we can explore together."

---

### B-005
**場景**：客戶說訂單很急，要求5天出貨，但正常生產週期是3週。
**策略**：誠實說明，提供替代方案。
**你的回應**：
> "We understand the urgency. Unfortunately, our standard lead time for this product is 21 days. 
> Options we can offer:
> 1. Rush production with 20% surcharge — reduces lead time to 14 days
> 2. Check if we have relevant semi-finished stock that can be expedited
> 3. Airfreight instead of sea freight — adds cost but saves ~20 days in transit
> Which option works best for you?"

---

### B-006
**場景**：客戶要求你提供「獨家代理」保護，承諾一年內只賣給他們這個市場。
**策略**：確認條件和量是否值得鎖定，需要法務介入。
**你的回應**：
> "We can consider exclusivity arrangements for significant committed volumes. Typically we'd require a minimum annual purchase commitment of USD [X] to justify exclusivity in your territory. 
> If this is of interest, I'll escalate to our management team and we can draft a formal distributor agreement. What volume can you realistically commit to?"

---

### B-007
**場景**：客戶要求在合約中加入「Penalty for late delivery」（逾期交貨罰款）條款，每天罰0.5%。
**策略**：需要評估是否接受，通常接受但設上限。
**你的回應**：
> "We can accept a late delivery penalty clause. We'd propose a cap of 5% of the order value, with a 5-day grace period for circumstances beyond our control (port congestion, force majeure). 
> This is standard in our OEM contracts. Shall I send you our template for reference?"

---

### B-008
**場景**：客戶想要改變已確認訂單的顏色規格。
**策略**：確認是否已投產，如已投產要收更換費。
**你的回應**：
> "Thank you for the update. I'll check the current production status immediately. 
> - If production hasn't started: We can accommodate the change at no extra charge with a [X]-day lead time extension.
> - If already in production: A re-work/restart fee of USD [X] will apply.
> I'll revert within the hour with the status."

---

### B-009
**場景**：客戶要求在PI上加入「subject to inspection approval」（待驗收才付款）條款。
**策略**：如果是在出貨前驗貨可以，如果是到港後驗貨才付最終款則風險太高。
**你的回應**：
> "We welcome pre-shipment inspection — you're welcome to appoint a third-party inspector (SGS, BV, Intertek) or visit our factory before shipment. 
> However, for the payment structure, we'd need to maintain 'balance before shipment' — once inspection passes, payment is made, then we ship. 
> 'Payment subject to inspection on arrival' is not feasible as it creates too much exposure for us."

---

### B-010
**場景**：客戶PO下得很急，但沒有附上技術圖紙，要你先確認再補。
**策略**：不能在沒有圖紙的情況下開始生產，否則做錯了誰負責？
**你的回應**：
> "I've received your PO — thank you. To proceed, we'll need the following before production can commence:
> 1. 2D drawing or 3D CAD file
> 2. Color standard/sample
> 3. Material specification
> Production lead time will start from the date we receive the complete technical package. Please send the files at your earliest convenience."

---

### B-011
**場景**：客戶說他們不需要原產地證書（C/O），你是否要主動提供？
**策略**：不需要主動增加成本，但告知客戶如果未來需要可以辦。
**你的回應**：
> "Understood. We'll proceed without C/O for this shipment, which will save the certificate cost. Please note that if you ever need it for customs duty reduction or LC compliance in the future, we can apply for it — just let us know at least 3 days before the sailing date."

---

### B-012
**場景**：客戶要一次訂10個不同料號，每個才100pcs，全部都低於你的MOQ。
**策略**：可以做，但要收小批量附加費，或合併到一個訂單談。
**你的回應**：
> "We can accommodate below-MOQ orders for multi-item orders. For this order of 10 SKUs × 100 pcs, we'd apply a combined small-order surcharge of 18%. 
> Alternatively, if you can increase any of the high-demand items to 300+ pcs, we can reduce the surcharge. Which items do you foresee higher demand for?"

---

### B-013
**場景**：客戶提出「consignment stock」（寄售庫存）要求：廣澤先備貨，客戶賣掉再付款。
**策略**：這對廠商極為不利，通常拒絕或設嚴格條件。
**你的回應**：
> "Consignment arrangements require significant capital and warehouse investment on our end. We typically don't offer this for new relationships. 
> As an alternative, we can offer a 'buffer stock' service — we maintain 4 weeks of safety stock at our factory, and you commit to placing call-off orders monthly. This gives you supply security without a consignment setup."

---

### B-014
**場景**：客戶詢問是否能做「私人品牌（White Label）」——產品跟你們一樣，但不打廣澤的牌子。
**你的回應**：
> "Yes, white-label manufacturing is available. We can remove our branding and apply your label or leave it unbranded. 
> Please note:
> 1. An NDA should be signed first
> 2. If you require custom packaging design, we can handle that for an additional tooling cost
> 3. MOQ and pricing remain the same as standard orders
> Shall I send you our NDA template to get started?"

---

### B-015
**場景**：客戶要求報一年的Long-term Agreement（LTA）/Annual Price，而不是每次報價。
**你的回應**：
> "We're open to establishing annual pricing for stable SKUs. To set up an LTA, we'd need:
> 1. Forecasted annual volume (by product)
> 2. Preferred payment terms
> 3. Desired delivery frequency
> Annual pricing benefits: locked-in price protection, production planning priority, and potential volume discount. Let's schedule a call to discuss the details?"

## C類：交期管理（Delivery / Lead Time）

### C-001
**場景**：工廠說原料缺貨，交期要延遲2週，但客戶要求下週出貨。
**策略**：主動告知，不要等客戶問，同時提出解決方案。
**你的回應**：
> "I need to inform you of an unexpected situation — our raw material supplier has a shortage that will delay our lead time by approximately 14 days. 
> Revised ETA: [date].
> To minimize impact:
> 1. We can partial-ship the in-stock portion immediately
> 2. We're exploring alternative material sources
> 3. Airfreight for the balance is available at our cost-share
> We sincerely apologize for this disruption. Please let me know how you'd like to proceed."

---

### C-002
**場景**：春節前客戶臨時下急單，要求節前出貨，但工廠即將停工。
**你的回應**：
> "Thank you for the urgent order. Our factory will close for Chinese New Year from [date] to [date]. 
> To ship before CNY, we'd need the order confirmed and deposit received by [date]. 
> If the timeline is too tight, the next available production slot would be in early [month]. 
> Strongly recommend confirming now to secure the pre-holiday slot as production capacity is limited."

---

### C-003
**場景**：客戶問「你們的標準交期是多久？」
**你的回應**：
> "Our standard lead times:
> - Standard catalog items: 15-20 working days from deposit received
> - Custom/new tooling items: 4-6 weeks for T1 mold + 20 days production
> - Reorder of existing products: 15 working days
> - Rush production: available with surcharge, 10-12 days
> Lead time starts from confirmed order + deposit receipt. Shall I check current production capacity for your specific items?"

---

### C-004
**場景**：客戶問能否提供「週進度報告」（weekly production update）。
**你的回應**：
> "Absolutely. We'll send you a weekly production status report every Friday, covering:
> - Order number and items
> - Production stage (raw material / in production / QC / ready to ship)
> - Confirmed shipping date
> Would you prefer email updates or do you use a sourcing platform we can update directly?"

---

### C-005
**場景**：客戶要求交期，但你不確定工廠能不能做到，要先查。
**你的回應**：

> Updated ETA: [date].
> The tracking link for real-time updates: [link if available]

> 4. Pre-qualified backup suppliers for critical raw materials

## D類：品質問題（Quality Issues）

### D-001
**場景**：客戶來信投訴，說收到的貨有顏色偏差（color deviation）。
**策略**：先道歉，再要求照片，再調查原因，再提出解決方案。
**你的回應**：
> "We're very sorry to hear about the color issue. This is not the standard we hold ourselves to.
> To investigate promptly:
> 1. Could you send photos of the affected pieces alongside the approved color standard?
> 2. Please note the box numbers/batch codes affected
> Once we receive the photos, we'll conduct an internal investigation and revert within 48 hours with our findings and proposed remedy (replacement, credit note, or rework)."

---

### D-002
**場景**：客戶說電鍍件有「起泡（blistering）」現象，大面積報廢，要求賠償。
**策略**：先確認是生產問題還是客戶儲存/使用問題。
**你的回應**：
> "We take this very seriously. Could you provide:
> 1. Photos/videos of the blistering issue
> 2. Total quantity affected and lot number
> 3. Storage conditions and any exposure to chemicals
> Our QC team will analyze whether this is a manufacturing defect or an environmental factor. We'll provide a root cause analysis (RCA) within 5 working days. 
> If confirmed as our defect, we'll issue a replacement or credit — whichever you prefer."

---

### D-003
**場景**：客戶投訴說有5%的產品尺寸超出公差，不能安裝。
**你的回應**：
> "I apologize for the dimensional non-conformance. Could you:
> 1. Confirm which specific dimension is out of tolerance and by how much
> 2. Send the affected pieces back as samples (or send photos with measurements)
> We'll 100% inspect the remaining inventory before any further shipments and provide a corrective action report (8D format if preferred). For the confirmed defective quantity, we'll arrange free replacements."

---

### D-004
**場景**：客戶說整個貨櫃到了，打開發現有10%的件有輕微劃傷，問能否接受折扣而不退貨。
**策略**：這是常見的談判，可以給折扣，但要確認劃傷等級。
**你的回應**：
> "Thank you for the photos. I've reviewed them with our QC manager. The scratches appear to be cosmetic — could you confirm if they're visible after installation in the vehicle?
> If these are acceptable for your end-use application, we can offer a credit of [X]% on the affected quantity.
> If the scratches affect function or appearance performance, we'll ship replacements in the next production batch.
> Which option would you prefer?"

---

### D-005
**場景**：客戶說收到的貨跟樣品不一樣，表面光澤度不同。
**策略**：先核對量產確認樣品（Counter Sample）是否有雙方簽字確認。
**你的回應**：
> "We'll compare the production samples against the approved counter sample in our records. Could you also send the sample you received alongside the production parts for comparison?
> Our QC process requires measuring gloss with a gloss meter (typically 60° reading). Please share your specification requirement — is there an acceptable gloss range (e.g., 30-40 GU)? We'll investigate and revert within 48 hours."

---

### D-006
**場景**：客戶要求做「PPAP（量產零件認可程序）」提交。
**你的回應**：
> "Yes, we can prepare a PPAP submission. Please confirm which level you require:
> - Level 1: Warrant only
> - Level 2: Warrant + limited supporting data
> - Level 3: Full PPAP (most common for Tier 1 OEM submissions)
> - Level 4/5: Specified by customer
> Our PPAP typically includes: Design Records, PFMEA, Control Plan, MSA, Capability Studies (Cpk), PSW (Part Submission Warrant). Estimated time: 3-4 weeks. Please share your customer-specific requirements."

---

### D-007
**場景**：客戶提出8D報告要求（根本原因分析）。
**你的回應**：
> "We'll prepare an 8D report addressing your concern. 
> Timeline:
> - D1-D3 (Team, Problem Description, Containment): Within 48 hours
> - D4-D6 (Root Cause, Permanent Corrective Actions): Within 5 working days
> - D7-D8 (Prevention, Closure): Within 10 working days
> We take quality escapes very seriously and will ensure the root cause is fully eliminated. I'll send you the D1-D3 report by [date]."
